Refund Policy

Effective July 16, 2026

Our products are digital and are delivered immediately. Because a downloaded file or access link cannot be returned, purchases are final once access has been provided. We still make things right when a purchase or delivery has a genuine problem.

When we provide a refund or replacement

Contact us within 14 days of purchase if:

  • the download or access link was not delivered;
  • the file is corrupted, defective, or cannot be accessed after reasonable troubleshooting;
  • you were charged more than once for the same purchase; or
  • the delivered product is materially different from its description at the time of purchase.

Depending on the issue, we will provide a working replacement, technical help, or a refund to the original payment method.

Change-of-mind requests

We do not provide refunds because a customer changed their mind, no longer needs the product, lacks compatible software, or did not review the product description before purchasing after the digital product has been delivered.

Requesting help

Use the merchant support contact shown on your order, payment, or delivery receipt and include the purchaser name, purchase date, product name, and a short description of the issue. Do not send full payment-card details.

Immediate digital delivery

At checkout, customers request immediate delivery and acknowledge that once access is provided, they lose any cancellation right that applicable law allows them to waive. If local consumer law gives you rights that cannot be waived, those rights still apply.

Chargebacks

Please contact us first so we can investigate delivery or billing issues promptly. This does not limit any rights available through your card issuer or under applicable law.
